## Step 1: Build the Frame ğŸ“¦
*Time: 30 minutes*

**You're making:** A rectangle like a picture frame

1. Lay your two 48" boards parallel, like train tracks
2. Space them exactly **14 inches apart** (inside to inside)
3. Put a 14" board across each end
4. **Pre-drill holes** (prevents splitting!)
5. Drive 2 screws through each corner
6. Add 2 more 14" braces in the middle

**âœ… Check:** Does it look like a ladder? Is it square?

4. Flip it right-side up

## Step 3: Make the Rollers ğŸ¢
*Time: 20 minutes*

**You're making:** Spinny tubes that the belt wraps around

1. Take a 16" dowel
2. Slide the 14" PVC pipe over it
3. Center it - dowel sticks out **1 inch on each side**
4. Drill a small pilot hole through PVC into dowel
5. Put a #8 screw in so they spin together
6. **Make 2 of these!**

**âœ… Check:** Does it spin freely when you hold the dowel ends?

## Step 4: Mount the Rollers ğŸ”©
*Time: 15 minutes*

**You're making:** Putting rollers in the frame so they can spin

1. At each short end, cut a **U-notch** in the cross brace
2. Notch size: **1.25" wide x 0.5" deep**
3. Make notch snug but let roller spin freely
4. Set each roller in place
5. Screw small wood blocks above to keep roller from popping out

**âœ… Check:** Both rollers spin easily without falling out?

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Belt slips | Tighten by moving tail roller back |
| Wobbles | Check legs are even, add bracing |
| Won't spin | Check motor wiring, VFD settings |
| Belt tracks crooked | Adjust roller alignment |
