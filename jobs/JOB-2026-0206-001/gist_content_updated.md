## üîß Build Steps (Simple Version)

### Step 1: Cut the Wood
Cut your 2x4 lumber into these pieces:
- 2 pieces at 48" (side rails)
- 4 pieces at 14" (cross braces) 
- 4 pieces at 30" (legs)

### Step 2: Build the Frame
1. Lay the 48" boards parallel, 14" apart
2. Put the 14" boards across the ends to make a rectangle
3. Add 2 more 14" boards in the middle for strength
4. Screw everything together (2 screws per corner)

### Step 3: Add the Legs  
1. Flip frame upside down
2. Screw a 30" leg at each corner
3. Flip back right-side up
4. Check that it doesn't wobble

### Step 4: Make the Rollers
1. Slide PVC pipe onto dowel (centered, with 1" sticking out each end)
2. Drill small hole through PVC into dowel
3. Put in a screw so they turn together
4. Make 2 of these

### Step 5: Install Rollers
1. Cut U-shaped notches in the end cross-braces
2. Set rollers in the notches
3. Make sure they spin freely

### Step 6: Add Motor Mount & Belt
1. Screw the 12"√ó12" plywood to one end
2. Wrap rubber belt around both rollers
3. Overlap the ends and glue or staple together
