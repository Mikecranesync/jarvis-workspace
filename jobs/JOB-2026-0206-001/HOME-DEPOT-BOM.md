## üîß ASSEMBLY ORDER

1. **Build rectangular frame** (48" x 14" inside)
2. **Attach legs** (36" tall = working height)
3. **Mount pillow blocks** at each end for roller shafts
4. **Install rollers** on shafts
5. **Add drive pulley** to motor shaft
6. **Add driven pulley** to drive roller shaft
7. **Install V-belt**
8. **Wrap belt material** around rollers, splice ends
9. **Tension belt** by sliding tail roller back
