## Competitors Analyzed

### 1. MaintainX (getmaintainx.com)
**Positioning:** "Bring unplanned equipment downtime to zero"

**Key Elements:**
- Hero: Strong pain-point headline + AI-powered messaging
- CTA: "No credit card required" (reduces friction)
- Sections:
  - Plan and execute work easily
  - Reduce unplanned downtime
  - Turn data into cost-saving insights
  - Produce more by keeping equipment online
  - Reduce regulatory risk
  - Eliminate parts shortages
- Social proof: "Leading enterprises rely on MaintainX"
- AI emphasis: AI-powered suggestions, reporting, predictions

**Differentiators:**
- Heavy AI messaging (6+ AI mentions)
- "Technician-loved usability with enterprise-grade AI"
- Multi-site management focus

---

### 2. Limble (limble.com)
**Positioning:** "#1 Rated by 50,000+ maintenance professionals"

**Key Elements:**
- Hero: Social proof headline (50,000+ users)
- Subhead: "Smarter maintenance, smoother operations"
- Features:
  - Work orders, simplified
  - Prevent downtime before it costs you
  - See the big picture with dashboards
  - Spend smarter on inventory
- Case study quote prominently featured
- Industry-specific pages (Manufacturing, Food & Beverage)

**Differentiators:**
- Rating/reviews as primary hook
- "Unplanned downtime costs 10x more than preventative"
- QR code-enabled work requests
- 24/7 support with <60 second response

## Common Patterns

### Headlines
| Company | Headline Style |
|---------|----------------|
| MaintainX | Pain point ("Bring downtime to zero") |
| Limble | Social proof ("#1 Rated by 50,000+") |
| UpKeep | Benefit ("Simplify maintenance") |

**Recommendation:** Use pain point headline with social proof support

### CTAs
- "Get Started Free" / "No credit card required"
- "Schedule Demo"
- "Calculate My Price"

**Recommendation:** "Join the Waitlist - First 50 get Founding Member pricing"

### Sections (in order)
1. Hero with headline + CTA
2. Feature highlights (3-4 cards)
3. Problem/solution details
4. Social proof (logos, testimonials)
5. Integration logos
6. Pricing or Demo CTA
7. Footer with resources

### Trust Signals
- Customer logos
- Star ratings (G2, Capterra)
- Testimonial quotes with photos
- Case study links
- Security/compliance badges

## FactoryLM Differentiation Opportunities

### What competitors DON'T emphasize:
1. **PLC/Equipment integration** - No one talks about actual machine connectivity
2. **AI that explains faults** - They have AI, but not diagnostic AI
3. **Smart glasses/wearables** - Completely missing from competitors
4. **Small team focus** - All target enterprise, not SMB

### Our unique value props:
1. **"AI that reads your equipment's mind"** - PLC Copilot angle
2. **"Built for the shop floor, not the boardroom"** - Technician-first
3. **"The CMMS that gets smarter with every repair"** - Learning system
4. **"Hands-free maintenance with smart glasses"** - Future vision

```
   "Be one of the first 50"

   Links, contact, social
```
