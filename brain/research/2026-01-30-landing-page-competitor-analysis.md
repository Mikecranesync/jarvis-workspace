## Executive Summary

Analyzed 3 major competitors in the industrial AI/CMMS space:
- **MaintainX** — Market leader, enterprise-focused
- **Fiix** — Rockwell-owned, case study driven
- **Factory AI** — Newer entrant, predictive focus

**Key Finding:** All competitors lead with **outcomes** (reduced downtime, cost savings) not features.

## Pattern Analysis

### What All Competitors Do

1. **Lead with outcomes, not features**
   - "Reduce downtime" not "AI-powered scheduling"
   - Numbers and percentages prominent

2. **Social proof above the fold**
   - Named customers with titles
   - Specific metrics (%, $, time saved)

3. **Free trial or no-credit-card demo**
   - Low friction entry point

4. **Enterprise security messaging**
   - "SOC 2 compliant"
   - "Custom permissions"
   - "Multi-site management"

### Differentiation Opportunities for FactoryLM

| Competitor Gap | FactoryLM Opportunity |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Generic AI messaging | **PLC-specific intelligence** |
| Enterprise-only pricing | **Free tier for small shops** |
| No technician perspective | **"By techs, for techs"** positioning |
| No video/AR | **Smart glasses integration** |
| SaaS-only | **Edge processing / privacy** |

## Recommended Landing Page Structure

Based on competitor patterns:

### Hero Section
```
Headline: [Outcome-focused, with number]
"Cut equipment downtime by 30% with AI that understands your PLCs"

Subhead: [How, briefly]
"The maintenance intelligence platform that learns from every work order"

CTA: "Start Free" (no credit card)
```

### Social Proof Bar
```
[Logo] [Logo] [Logo]
"Trusted by 50+ industrial facilities"
```

### Value Props (3-4 max)
```
1. Predict failures before they happen
2. Get instant diagnostics from fault codes
3. Build institutional knowledge automatically
```

### Case Study Section
```
"30% reduction in unplanned downtime"
— [Real customer name], Maintenance Manager
```

### Pricing (if transparent)
```
Free: 1 user, basic features
Pro: $X/user/month
Enterprise: Contact us
```
