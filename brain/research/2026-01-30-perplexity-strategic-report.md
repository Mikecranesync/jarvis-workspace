## Pricing Model for SMB Manufacturers

### Competitive Benchmarking

Based on 20+ CMMS providers analyzed:

| Tier | Price Range | Typical Features |
|---|---|---|
| Free/Starter | $0 (limited) | 2-5 work orders/month, basic tracking |
| Basic | $20-30/user/month | Preventive maintenance, mobile, limited integrations |
| Professional | $45-75/user/month | Unlimited work orders, analytics, API |
| Enterprise | $100-150+/user/month | Custom integrations, dedicated support, advanced AI |

**Key Insight:** Market expects freemium entry with paid plans at $45-75/user/month.

### Recommended Pricing Architecture for PLC Copilot

| Plan | Price | Diagnoses/Month | Features | Target |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 5 | Basic diagnostics, email support | Lead gen, evaluation |
| **Technician** | $49/user | 50 | Full database, SMS, 24hr support | Solo techs, contractors |
| **Professional** | $99/user | Unlimited | Priority support (<4hr), analytics, custom KB | Teams (3-10 users) |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Unlimited | API, ERP integrations, dedicated AM | Large mfg (10+ users) |

### Pricing Rationale

1. **Free Tier Drives Adoption:** 5 diagnoses/month lets technicians experience value, builds diagnostic database (data moat), generates leads
2. **$49 Positioning:** Above UpKeep Lite ($20) but below MaintainX/Fiix ($45). Premium justified through AI specialization and mobile-first UX
3. **$99 Professional Tier:** Captures teams willing to pay for "unlimited" and better support. At 10 users = $990/month per customerâ€”strong unit economics
4. **Custom Enterprise:** Once you have 10+ SMB customers and proven ROI, approach larger manufacturers with volume pricing ($79/user for 25+, $69/user for 50+)

### Alternative Model: Usage-Based Pricing

If your ICP leans toward independent contractors or sporadic users:
- **Pay-Per-Diagnosis:** $1-2 per diagnosis
- **Pre-Paid Credits:** $50 for 100 diagnoses ($0.50 each)

| Model | Pros | Cons |
|-------|------|------|
| Usage-based | Lower barrier, pay-as-you-go, usage = value | Unpredictable revenue, discourages habit |
| Subscription | Predictable revenue, encourages usage | Higher barrier to entry |

**Hybrid Approach:** Offer bothâ€”let customers choose.

### Pricing Psychology & Experimentation

- **Anchor High:** Show Enterprise pricing even without customers. Makes $99/month seem reasonable
- **Annual Discount:** 2 months free for annual prepay (16% discount)â€”improves cash flow, reduces churn
- **Pilot Pricing:** $99-299/month becomes "Founder's Rate" for first 20 customersâ€”creates urgency
- **Test & Iterate:** After 10 customers, analyze which tier has highest LTV and lowest churn

### Founder-Led Sales: Your Permanent Responsibility

Don't hire salespeople prematurely. Founders must lead sales through at least the first 20-50 customers.

**Why Founder-Led Sales Matters:**
1. **Deep Product Knowledge:** You understand nuances no rep will grasp initially
2. **Credibility:** Prospects trust founder's passion and expertise
3. **Feedback Loop:** Direct conversations inform product decisions
4. **Pricing Flexibility:** You can negotiate and experiment on the fly

**Phases of Founder-Led Sales:**
- Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Founder does ALL sales
- Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Founder documents repeatable process
- Phase 3 (Year 2): First sales hire, founder trains
- Phase 4 (Year 2-3): Founder focuses on high-value accounts
- Phase 5+: Hire sales leader, shift to strategic oversight

**Critical Mistake to Avoid:** Don't hire a salesperson before you've personally closed 20+ deals and documented a repeatable process. Sales hires without founder-validated playbooks fail 80%+ of the time.
