## Products

### Predict (Anomaly Detection)
- Machine learning-based anomaly detection
- Real-time asset monitoring
- Works with sensors, historians, PLCs
- **Pricing:** Starting $500/monitoring point (volume discounts)

### Prevent (CMMS)
- AI-powered work order generation
- Asset management
- Maintenance scheduling
- **Pricing:** Free tier available, $35/user/month business
