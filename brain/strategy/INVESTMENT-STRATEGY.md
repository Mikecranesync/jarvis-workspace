## 1. YC Standard Terms 2025-2026

### Current YC Deal Structure
- **Total Investment:** $500,000
- **Fixed Equity:** $125,000 for 7% (implying $1.78M post-money valuation)
- **SAFE Component:** $375,000 on uncapped MFN (Most Favored Nation) SAFE
- **Pro rata rights:** Yes
- **Post-money SAFE:** Standard since 2018 update

### YC Fundraising Timeline
**Typical Post-YC Pattern:**
- **Demo Day → 3 months:** Seed conversations begin
- **Demo Day → 6-9 months:** Seed round closes ($2-8M typical)
- **12-18 months post-YC:** Series A readiness
- **Median Series A:** $15-25M at $60-120M post-money

### YC Application Strategy for FactoryLM

- Clear path to revenue through CMMS integration

**Weaknesses to address:**

## 3. FactoryLM-Specific Positioning Strategy

### A. Pre-Revenue vs Post-Revenue Decision
**Recommendation: Target $50K-100K ARR before major fundraising**

**Pre-Revenue Approach (Current State):**
- Valuation range: $8-15M pre-money
- Emphasize: Demo, technical proof, market size
- Investor type: Early-stage VCs, industrial angels
- Risk: Higher dilution, longer sales cycles

**Post-Revenue Approach (Target):**
- Valuation range: $15-25M pre-money  
- Timeline: 6-9 months to reach $50K ARR
- Strategy: 3-5 pilot customers at $10-20K annually
- Benefits: 2x higher valuation, easier investor conversations

### B. Solo Founder Considerations
**Challenge:** Only 17% of funded startups have solo founders
**Mitigation Strategies:**
1. **Advisory board:** Recruit 3-4 industrial experts as advisors
2. **Technical co-founder search:** Allocate 20% equity pool for future technical co-founder
3. **Early key hires:** Show hiring plan for CTO within 6 months of funding
4. **Proof of execution:** Demonstrate ability to ship and sell alone

### C. Demo-Stage Fundraising Approach
**Phase 1: Demo Refinement (Months 1-2)**
- Polish Factory I/O integration demo
- Create customer-facing product demo (5-min video)
- Build simple landing page with demo request
- Document technical architecture

**Phase 2: Customer Discovery (Months 3-4)**
- Target 50 maintenance managers for interviews
- Validate problem/solution fit
- Generate 10-15 letters of intent
- Price validation: $10-20K annual contracts

**Phase 3: Pilot Sales (Months 5-7)**
- Convert 3-5 letters to paid pilots
- Achieve $25-50K ARR
- Document case studies and ROI metrics
- Prepare investor deck

**Phase 4: Fundraising (Months 8-12)**
- Approach seed investors
- Demonstrate traction and growth trajectory
- Target $3-5M round at $15-20M pre-money
