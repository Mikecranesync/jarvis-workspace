## Key Strategic Insights

### The Big Realization
> "Have I just discovered the way to put any machine online and have it be analyzed by AI for $30?"

**Yes.** ESP32 + FactoryLM = universal machine connectivity at 10-100x lower cost than enterprise alternatives.

### Competitive Moat
1. **Air-gapped** - No cloud dependency (defense, pharma, critical infra)
2. **Edge-first** - Competitors are cloud-first, 3-5 years behind
3. **Price** - $30/node vs $500+ competitors
4. **LLM Interface** - Natural language queries to factory

### Target Markets
- Small/medium manufacturers (underserved by Rockwell/Siemens)
- Security-conscious (defense, pharma)
- Air-gapped requirements
- International (Latin America, SE Asia)

### Safety & Ethics Discussion
- Read-only by default (no write to PLCs)
- One-way data flow
- Supplement human judgment, don't replace
- Liability considerations discussed
- IEC 62443 compliance path
