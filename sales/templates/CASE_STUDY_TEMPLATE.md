## Template Notes

### One-Pager Version
To create a one-page executive summary, include only:
- Header section
- Executive Summary with key results
- The Challenge (1-2 sentences)
- Results table
- Customer testimonial
- Contact information

### Design Tips
- Use emojis sparingly but consistently for section headers
- Bold key metrics and improvements
- Use tables for before/after comparisons
- Include customer logo in final version
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences max)
- Use bullet points for easy scanning
