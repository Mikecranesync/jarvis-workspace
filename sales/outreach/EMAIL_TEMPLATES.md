## Personalization Variables

### Required for Each Email:
- **[FIRST_NAME]** - Prospect's first name
- **[COMPANY_NAME]** - Target company
- **[SPECIFIC_PLC_TYPE]** - Allen-Bradley/Siemens model if known
- **[SPECIFIC_PROCESS]** - Manufacturing/packaging/chemical/etc.
- **[INDUSTRY]** - Food & bev/pharma/manufacturing

### Nice-to-Have:
- **[SPECIFIC_PAIN]** - Recent plant issues from LinkedIn/news
- **[SIMILAR_COMPANY]** - Reference customer in same industry
- **[SIMILAR_COMPANY_SIZE]** - Employee count reference
- **[CASE_STUDY_LINK]** - Industry-specific success story

### Research Sources:
- Company website (equipment, processes)
- LinkedIn posts (maintenance challenges)
- Industry news (recent shutdowns, expansions)
- Trade publications (plant manager interviews)

---

## Timing & Cadence

- **Email 1:** Monday-Wednesday, 8-10 AM local time
- **Email 2:** Thursday morning (3 business days later)
- **Email 3:** Tuesday morning (7 calendar days from initial)

**Best performing days:** Tuesday-Thursday  
**Avoid:** Mondays (catching up), Fridays (weekend mode)

---

## A/B Testing Ideas

### Subject Lines:
- Question format vs. Stat/benefit
- Company name vs. Industry reference
- Pain-focused vs. Solution-focused

### Email Length:
- Long-form (current) vs. Short-form (under 100 words)
- Case study details vs. High-level benefits

### Call-to-Action:
- "Worth a conversation?" vs. "15-minute call?"
- Calendar link vs. "Reply to this email"
- Specific times vs. "When works for you?"
