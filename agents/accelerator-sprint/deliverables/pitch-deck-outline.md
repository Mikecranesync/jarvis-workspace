## Appendix: Design Guidelines

### Visual Consistency
- **Color Palette:** Industrial blues (#1E3A8A), safety orange (#F97316), clean whites
- **Typography:** Bold headers, clean sans-serif (Inter/Roboto)
- **Style:** Technical precision with human accessibility

### Image Sources
- Real factory photos from Mike's experience
- PLC hardware screenshots from current integration
- Custom diagrams for architecture and competitive positioning
- Professional headshots in industrial settings

### Key Messages to Reinforce
1. **Inside vs Outside:** We're native to PLCs, not bolted on
2. **$30 vs $500K:** Democratizing AI for all factories, not just Fortune 500
3. **Recursive Learning:** AI becomes code, reducing future AI dependency
4. **Domain Expertise:** Built by manufacturing engineers who understand the problem

### Presentation Tips
- Use factory sounds/ambient noise for authenticity
- Demo real PLC integration if possible
- Emphasize human-AI collaboration story (9,554 messages)
- Connect to broader Industry 4.0 / Manufacturing 2025 narrative

**Demo Time:** 2 minutes showing live PLC integration
**Q&A Prep:** Focus on scalability, competitive response, technical depth
