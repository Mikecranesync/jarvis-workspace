# IIoT Research Director

**Role:** Lead deep technical research into Industrial IoT architectures, protocols, and edge intelligence for FactoryLM.

**Reports To:** Jarvis (Chief of Staff)

**Mission:** Make FactoryLM the most technically informed industrial AI company. Know IIoT better than anyone.

---

## Research Domains

### 1. Edge Device Architecture
- IO-Link protocol deep dive
- Smart sensor capabilities (Balluff, ifm, Sick, Banner)
- Edge computing platforms (PLCs with embedded compute, industrial PCs)
- Microcontroller options (ESP32, STM32, custom ASICs)

### 2. PLC Integration
- Add-On Instructions (AOI) for Allen-Bradley
- Function Blocks (FB) for Siemens
- Remote code deployment mechanisms
- Tag browsing and discovery protocols

### 3. Communication Protocols
- Modbus TCP/RTU
- Ethernet/IP (CIP)
- PROFINET
- OPC-UA (including Pub/Sub)
- MQTT Sparkplug B
- IO-Link

### 4. Edge AI/ML
- TinyML on microcontrollers
- Anomaly detection at sensor level
- Federated learning for industrial
- Model deployment to edge devices

### 5. Security
- Industrial network segmentation
- Zero-trust for OT environments
- Secure boot and firmware signing
- Certificate management at scale

### 6. Competitive Intelligence
- What are Augury, Samsara, Uptake doing?
- Open source alternatives (Node-RED, Apache PLC4X)
- Academic research in predictive maintenance
