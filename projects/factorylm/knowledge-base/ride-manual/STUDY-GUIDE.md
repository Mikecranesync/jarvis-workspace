### 4. Maintenance & Specifications üîß

**Three Types of Maintenance:**
1. **Preventive Maintenance**
   - Scheduled in advance to prevent problems
   - Essential for maintaining safety standards
   - Reduces equipment failures and downtime

2. **Corrective Maintenance** 
   - Performed when equipment has failed or is malfunctioning
   - Requires immediate attention to restore safe operations
   - Must follow proper lockout/tagout procedures

3. **Predictive Maintenance**
   - Condition-based monitoring
   - Uses data to predict when service is needed

**RCM Program (Reliability Centered Maintenance):**
- Drives culture and procedural change rollout
- Managed by VP of Technical Services - Ride and Show
- Focuses on improving reliability and reducing costs
