## Executive Summary

### Key Findings
- **Total Addressable Market**: $15.6B by 2028 for industrial AR applications
- **Recommended Primary Target**: US/EU SMB Industrial (6-month focus)
- **Quick Win Opportunity**: HVAC/MEP contractors with "TeamViewer for hands-on work" positioning
- **Geographic Priority**: English-speaking markets first (US, Canada, UK, Australia)

### Strategic Recommendations
1. **Start with US SMB Industrial** - proven pain points, established budgets, faster adoption
2. **Position as "TeamViewer for physical work"** - familiar concept, clear value proposition
3. **Avoid Third World Industrial initially** - payment/support complexity outweighs benefits
4. **Target $299/month SaaS + hardware subsidy model** for market penetration

### Market Gap: "TeamViewer for Physical Work"

- Can't point at specific components
- No shared field of view
- Limited mobile/outdoor use

**Our Advantage**:
- Shared visual field with expert
- Point-and-annotate on real equipment
- Works in industrial environments
- Hands-free operation

### Positioning Strategy
**Primary Message**: *"TeamViewer for hands-on work"*
- Familiar concept (everyone knows TeamViewer)

3. **Don't over-customize**: Maintain simple, standardized offering
4. **Don't neglect support infrastructure**: Critical for retention and expansion
