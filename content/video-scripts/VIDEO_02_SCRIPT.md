### Music Strategy:
- **Opening:** Metallica-style heavy metal for energy and brand
- **Technical sections:** Industrial electronic music with strong beat
- **Demo sections:** Tension-building music for drama
- **Educational sections:** Cleaner tech music for focus
- **Outro:** Return to metal theme for brand consistency

### Visual Style:
- **Color scheme:** Dark backgrounds with bright accent colors (blues, greens, reds)
- **Typography:** Bold, industrial fonts for callouts
- **Transitions:** Network/data flow animations between sections
- **Pacing:** Quick cuts during high-energy sections, longer holds for technical explanations

### Technical Presentation:
- **Screen recordings:** Clean, high-resolution captures of actual interfaces
- **Animations:** Smooth, professional motion graphics for architecture
- **Data visualization:** Clear, readable charts and metrics
- **Code display:** Syntax-highlighted, properly formatted code snippets

### Engagement Elements:
- **Dramatic reveals:** Build anticipation before showing results
- **Progress indicators:** Show processing time, completion rates
- **Before/after comparisons:** Highlight improvements clearly
- **Call-out boxes:** Emphasize key metrics and achievements

### Audio Considerations:
- **Industrial sounds:** Layer in subtle machinery sounds during factory footage
- **Notification sounds:** Use for agent alerts and completions
- **Transition sounds:** Tech-style whooshes and data transfer sounds
- **Voice processing:** Consider slight audio processing for "AI voice" sections
