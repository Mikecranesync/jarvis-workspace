## EDITING NOTES
- **Pacing:** Keep cuts snappy during problem section, slow down for technical explanations
- **Music:** Industrial/electronic for drama, cleaner tech music for solution sections
- **Graphics:** Use clean, modern UI elements that contrast with chaotic industrial footage
- **Color scheme:** Industrial blues/grays for problems, bright greens/blues for solutions
- **Transitions:** Use data/network visualizations as transition elements
- **Audio:** Layer in subtle industrial sounds (machinery, alarms) during problem sections
