## Validation Interview Questions

**Discovery (5 min):**
1. "Walk me through the last time a PLC error code stopped production. What happened?"
2. "How long did it take to diagnose? What resources did you use?"

**Pain Quantification (5 min):**
3. "How much does one hour of unplanned downtime cost your operation?"
4. "How often do unfamiliar PLC errors occur? Weekly? Monthly?"

**Current Solution (3 min):**
5. "What tools or resources do your techs currently use to diagnose PLC issues?"
6. "Any software you've tried for this? What worked/didn't work?"

**Solution Validation (5 min):**
7. "If you could text a photo of a PLC error and get diagnosis + fix steps in 60 seconds, what would that be worth to you?"
8. "Would you pay $99/month per technician for a tool like that?"

**Close (2 min):**
9. "I'm building exactly this tool. Would you be open to a 15-minute demo?"
10. "Who else should I talk to about this problem?"
