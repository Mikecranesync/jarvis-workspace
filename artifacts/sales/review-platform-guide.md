## G2 Setup

### Create Vendor Account
1. Go to https://seller.g2.com/
2. Click "Claim Your Product" or "Add Your Product"
3. Complete company verification

### Product Listing Details

**Category:** Maintenance Management Software, CMMS

**G2 Grid Positioning:**
- Early stage = Focus on "High Performer" quadrant

**Description:** Same as Capterra (adjust formatting)

3. Can offer incentives (gift cards) within G2 guidelines
