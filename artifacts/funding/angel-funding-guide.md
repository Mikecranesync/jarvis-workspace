## AngelList Profile Setup Steps

### Step 1: Create Personal Account
1. Go to AngelList (now Wellfound)
2. Click "Sign in" → Enter email
3. Verify with code sent to email
4. Set password, add full name
5. Enable two-factor authentication

### Step 2: Company Incorporation (If not done)
AngelList partners with Stripe Atlas for Delaware C-Corp:
- ~10 minute application
- Stripe handles: incorporation docs, EIN, equity issuance, 83(b) elections
- EIN received in 1-3 business days
- Free Starter Plan for startups under $1M raised

### Step 3: Build Company Profile
Required fields:
- **Company Name:** FactoryLM
- **Mission:** AI-powered diagnostics for industrial maintenance teams
- **Industry:** Industrial AI / Manufacturing Tech
- **Stage:** Pre-seed
- **Location:** [Mike's location]
- **Team:** Link founder profiles

### Step 4: Optimize for Visibility
- Add product screenshots/demo video
- Post jobs (increases visibility)
- Get featured (top 2% of profiles)
- AngelList recommends investors based on market, stage, location
