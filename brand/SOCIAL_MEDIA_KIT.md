## Profile Picture Specifications

### Universal Standards
- **Shape**: Square with circular crop considerations
- **Safe Zone**: Central 80% for critical elements (accounts for circular crops)
- **Style**: High contrast, industrial aesthetic
- **Readability**: Must be clear at 32x32px minimum size

### Platform-Specific Sizes

#### YouTube
- **Dimensions**: 800x800px
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 4MB
- **Display**: 98x98px in most contexts

#### Twitter/X
- **Dimensions**: 400x400px
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or GIF
- **File Size**: Under 2MB
- **Display**: Variable, 128x128px common

#### LinkedIn
- **Dimensions**: 400x400px
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 8MB
- **Display**: 268x268px on profile

#### TikTok
- **Dimensions**: 200x200px minimum
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 20MB
- **Display**: Variable sizing

#### Instagram
- **Dimensions**: 320x320px minimum
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 30MB
- **Display**: Various sizes

#### Discord/Twitch
- **Dimensions**: 128x128px minimum
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or GIF
- **File Size**: Under 8MB (Discord), 10MB (Twitch)
- **Special**: Animated GIF support

### Profile Picture Concept

#### Primary Design: "The Controller"
A close-up of a robotic/mechanical hand gripping a sophisticated control device, with subtle electric blue LED accents and circuit pattern details visible in the background.

**Design Elements**:
- **Central Focus**: Robotic hand (60% of image)
- **Control Device**: Industrial joystick or command interface
- **Background**: Dark with subtle circuit patterns
- **Accent Color**: Electric Blue LED highlights
- **Text**: None (relies on username for identification)
