# MASTER OF PUPPETS - YouTube Channel Art Specifications

## Technical Requirements

### Dimensions & Safe Zones
- **Full Canvas**: 2560x1440 pixels
- **TV Safe Area**: 2560x423 pixels (center, full width)
- **Desktop Safe Area**: 2560x423 pixels (center)
- **Tablet Safe Area**: 1855x423 pixels (center)
- **Mobile Safe Area**: 1546x423 pixels (center)
- **Minimum Safe Area**: 1235x423 pixels (center) - CRITICAL CONTENT ONLY

### File Specifications
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Maximum 6MB
- **Color Space**: sRGB
- **DPI**: 72 (web optimized)

## Device-Specific Layout Zones

### Universal Safe Zone (1235x423px center)
**Must contain ALL critical information**
- Channel name/logo
- Primary tagline
- Upload schedule
- Essential contact information

### Extended Safe Zone (1546x423px center)
**Additional content for larger mobile screens**
- Subtitle or extended tagline
- Social media icons
- Secondary branding elements

### Desktop Zone (2560x423px center)
**Full desktop experience**
- Complete brand message
- Detailed visual elements
- Extended background design
- Social proof elements

### TV Zone (2560x423px center)
**Living room viewing**
- Large, readable text
- High contrast elements
- Simplified visual hierarchy
- Maximum brand impact

## Master Design Concept

### Theme: "The Control Room"
A high-tech command center aesthetic that positions the viewer as entering the Master of Puppets' domain - a place where complex systems are orchestrated with precision and creativity.

### Visual Narrative
The channel art depicts a sophisticated control environment where automation mastery happens. The design suggests both the precision of industrial control systems and the creative potential of human-guided technology.

## Typography Specifications

### Channel Name
- **Font**: Rajdhani Bold (700)
- **Size**: 84px (scales down responsively)
- **Color**: Plasma White (#F7FAFC)
- **Effect**: Subtle glow effect in Electric Blue
- **Shadow**: 3px drop shadow at 60% opacity

### Primary Tagline
- **Font**: Inter SemiBold (600)
- **Size**: 32px
- **Color**: Chrome Silver (#8B9DC3)
- **Spacing**: Letter-spacing +2px for impact

### Secondary Text

- **Size**: 24px
- **Color**: Machine Gray (#4A5568)
- **Usage**: Upload schedule, contact info

### Technical Accents

- **Usage**: Version numbers, tech specs
